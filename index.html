Problem 2: The "First Year Bolus"

Objection

  • Initial screening = catch-up
  • Year 1 finds MORE positives
  • Triggers imaging, biopsies
  • Year 1 costs SPIKE

Reframe

  • Those cancers were coming
  • Stage 1 Year 1 vs Stage 3 Year 3

"Would you rather find Stage 1 now, or Stage 3 in three years?"

Pricing Strategy

Blended vs. unbundled

Two Pricing Models

Blended Rate

$1,250

per eligible member

  • Whole genome sequencing
  • Genetic counselor
  • 24 months navigation
  • Family cascade testing

Simple, predictable

Unbundled

$710 + $2,840 + $90/mo

variable by positive rate

  • $710 = test (everyone)
  • $2,840 = positive upfront
  • $90/mo × 24 = navigation

Complex, variable

The Math: Blended vs. Unbundled

Assuming 15.5% positive rate across 100 members:

ModelCalculationTotalPer Member
Blended100 × $1,250$125,000$1,250
Unbundled100×$710 + 15.5×($2,840+$2,160)~$148,500~$1,485

Insight: Blended is cheaper for employer. NoviHealth takes engagement risk.
